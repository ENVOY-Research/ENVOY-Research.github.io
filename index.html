Research

Welcome. We're a group of technical researchers at MIT Lincoln Laboratory. Our project is ENVOY: Enabling Natural-language Opportunity & Versatility. With support from a growing list of collaborators, our group explores questions surrounding improving the machine translation of American Sign Language (ASL).

Achieving full ASL translation requires interdisciplinary cooperation. The fields of machine learning, computer vision, human-computer interaction, and linguistics (to name a few) must integrate fluidly. Our group intends to bridge implicit gaps of domain-specific understanding, and to build comprehensive translatory system designs for researchers to aim towards.
